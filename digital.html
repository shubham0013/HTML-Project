About

The year 2014 witnessed the onset of a skyrocketing pace of development in our country since the inception of Shri. Narendra Modi as the Prime Minister. The Digital India campaign’s signature catchphrase “Power to Empower” speaks volumes in itself. With this endeavour, India will mark its territory on the digital map of the world.

It encourages the youth, who are the core of future development to participate in the process wholeheartedly with the vision of making digital infrastructure easily available to every citizen for a cashless economy. Technozion aspires to contribute to the mission at the grassroot level by promoting cashless payments in college facilities and creating awareness on the benefits of a digital future.
